# tasqs

A terminal-native task & note manager where humans (ratatui TUI) and AI agents (scriptable subcommands) work against the same human-readable JSONL data.

Design docs (PRDs, issues, ADRs) live in `docs/`, which is **gitignored** — they are local-only and not on GitHub. `CONTEXT.md` is the canonical vocabulary; `PRODUCT.md` is the product brief.
